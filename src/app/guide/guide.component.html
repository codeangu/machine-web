Helpful Tips
  • A green message at the top-right means an action worked. A red one means something needs fixing.
  • You cannot delete a product, customer or supplier that already has sales/purchases — delete those records first.
  • Stock updates by itself on every purchase, sale, and return — you rarely need manual adjustment.
  • Use the search box on any list page to quickly find an invoice, product, or person.
